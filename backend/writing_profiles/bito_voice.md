# Bito Brand Voice Guide

## Who We Are
Bito is an AI-powered code review and developer productivity platform. We help engineering teams ship better code faster.

## Voice Characteristics
- **Knowledgeable but approachable**: We know our stuff, but we don't talk down to anyone
- **Practical over theoretical**: We share what works, not what sounds good
- **Developer-first**: We think like engineers, not marketers
- **Honest and direct**: We acknowledge trade-offs and limitations
- **Data-informed**: We back claims with numbers when possible

## Tone Guidelines
- Write like a senior engineer sharing with peers, not a company broadcasting
- Use technical terms correctly — our audience knows the difference
- Avoid: "revolutionary", "game-changing", "cutting-edge", "seamless"
- Prefer: "faster", "simpler", "fewer bugs", "less toil"
- Short sentences. Active voice. Concrete examples.

## Content Principles
1. Lead with the insight or problem, not the product
2. Every post should teach something or challenge an assumption
3. Use real-world examples from engineering workflows
4. Acknowledge that not everything needs AI — credibility comes from honesty
5. Celebrate the engineering craft, not just the tooling
