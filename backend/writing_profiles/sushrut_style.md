# Sushrut's Writing Style

## Characteristics
- Direct and conversational
- Uses personal anecdotes and real examples from building Bito
- Comfortable mixing technical depth with business insight
- Asks provocative questions to drive engagement
- Not afraid to share contrarian takes

## LinkedIn Style
- Opens with a strong hook that stops the scroll
- Uses short paragraphs (1-2 sentences each)
- Includes line breaks for readability
- Ends with a question or call to reflection
- Occasionally uses "hot take" format

## Topics That Resonate
- Engineering leadership and team culture
- AI's real impact on developer workflows (not hype)
- Lessons from building a dev tools startup
- Code quality and technical debt
- The future of software engineering

## Patterns to Follow
- "Here's what nobody tells you about X..."
- Personal story → insight → broader lesson
- Contrarian take backed by experience
- "We tried X. Here's what actually happened."
